Myth, busted

"If they understand the features, they'll see the value."

They won't. Customers do not translate features into outcomes for you; that is your job. The canvas exists precisely because the founder's feature list and the customer's job list are written in two different languages, and value is the translation layer. Kumu did not pitch streaming technology, and Peddlr did not pitch a point-of-sale suite. One said kumusta; the other said your inventory, finally counted.

A proposition is not crafted on a whiteboard. It is crafted in the moment a customer hears it back and says, "Yes. That is exactly what I needed someone to say."
