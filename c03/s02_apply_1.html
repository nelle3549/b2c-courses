"I dread the Friday receipt pile. It eats my only free evening."

Pain: an active frustration the customer wants to escape while doing their job. The word "dread" is the tell.

"I'd love to have Saturday mornings free for my kids."

Gain: a positive outcome the customer wants to enable. Not the absence of a pain, a new good thing they are reaching for.
