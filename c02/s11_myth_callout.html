Myth, busted

"If I just build it, the problem will reveal itself."

No. What reveals itself is whether the problem matters enough to pay for. By the time you've built, you've already paid the cost in cash, time, and team morale. Kalibrr paid that tuition in a year and ten teammates. The cheap version of the same learning is empathy work before shipping. The expensive version is shipping and watching nobody care.

Enthusiasm makes founders ship. Evidence makes them ship the right thing. You are now allowed to be excited, after you've done the listening.
