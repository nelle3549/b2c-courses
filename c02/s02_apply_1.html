Which mindset is speaking?

Tap one answer per row. Wrong guesses are fine. They teach more.

"Uso na ang AI! Let's build an AI study-buddy app before someone else does."

Starts from a technology trend, not a named person's pain. Nobody in this sentence has a problem yet.

"Three of the five stall owners I talked to said they lose track of utang. I want to dig into that."

A pattern observed across real conversations, and the next move is to dig, not to build. Textbook problem-first.

"Every business needs a website, so barbershops will surely pay for our site builder."

"Needs" is asserted, not observed. No barbershop owner has been watched, asked, or even met in this sentence.

"I watched Aling Rosa close her sari-sari store: she counted the coins three times and still wrote the total on the notebook cover."

Pure observed behavior, specific to one real evening. This is the raw material underlying problems are made of.
