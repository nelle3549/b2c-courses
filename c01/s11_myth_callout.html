Myth, busted

"You need to be in Manila, and funded, to start a startup."

Neither is true, and the law says so. RA 11337's definition begins at "any person": no company, no capital, no capital city required. DOST-PCIEERD funds TBIs across the regions (USTP and MSU-IIT in Mindanao, USC and UPV in the Visayas, MMSU in Northern Luzon), and grant recipients have run whole funding cycles without leaving their province. Packworks piloted with five sari-sari stores; Investagrams scaled on reinvested earnings and free ad credits. The bottleneck was never location or money. It's whether your problem statement is sharp enough for a panel to parse, and whether you've walked into the rooms where that sharpening happens.

The ecosystem looks empty from the outside and crowded from the inside. Your card names your idea, your verdict, and your door. The work of this course was to move you one step inside; the commitment on your card is that step.
